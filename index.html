See growth across investments and asset classes with modern data visualisations and AI chat analysis.

What is ROI Master?

A fully client-side investment analysis dashboard covering 300+ global assets across Stocks, ETFs, Commodities, and Real Estate — with returns at 1, 5, 10, 15, and 20-year horizons. No login, no backend, no tracking. Just data.

Hover any KPI tile or data visualisation and hit Analyse with AI — the chatbot fires a contextual question instantly and answers in plain English, drawing a live chart inside the reply whenever the data calls for one.

7 Interactive Charts Bar, heatmap, donut, line & scatter — every one has a one-click AI analysis button
AI Chat That Draws Its Own Charts Ask anything — the AI responds in plain English and renders a live bar chart inside the chat bubble whenever it lists ranked or comparable data
AI-Generated Data Tiles Hover any KPI tile — best performers, outliers, club counts, medians — and hit Analyse with AI for an instant AI-powered breakdown of that exact stat
Voice Readout (Web Speech API) Every AI reply is read aloud in a natural female voice. Toggle the speaker icon in the chat header to mute or unmute — preference is saved between visits
300+ Assets Stocks, ETFs, Commodities, Real Estate — easily add your own via CSV upload
Open Source MIT licensed — fork, adapt, and deploy freely on Netlify
